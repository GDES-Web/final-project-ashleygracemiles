Welcome to Meowfest 2023!

California's Largest Cat Music Festival is back!

About

Meowfest is a one-of-a-kind music festival that celebrates all things feline. This exciting event brings together cat lovers from all over to enjoy live music, art installations, and unique vendors, all while raising awareness for cat adoption and welfare.
